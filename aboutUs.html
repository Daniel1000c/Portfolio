About Anime Oasis

Mission Statement

Our mission here at Anime Oasis is to give the most exciting experience that a user can have when visiting our online shop. We strive for excellent customer service and shipping process for our beloved customers.Furthermore, we always strive to keep our items authenticate and never settle for bootleg products.

History

Anime Oasis started back in April 2020 when the pandemic hit. I wanted to get a place that I was able to shop to since I couldn't go outside for a couple of months.First, me and my small team started out in a warehouse where we started to go with small shipments and expanded from there. Here at Anime Oasis, we strive ourselves with excellent customer service and exceptional delivery time of your product.

Vision and Culture

Here at Anime Oasis we strive to have a peaceful company culture and helps to rely on one another when in need. Here at the Anime Oasis family try to provide a friendly and welcoming environment where all future applicants can work. We offer one hour lunch breaks and thirty minute breaks when needed. Furthermore, we make sure to comply with local wage values in our area.

Why Choose Anime Oasis?

Why choose us over any other competition? Im glad you asked. Here at Anime Oasis, we pride ourselves to have rare limited-edition products that I can guarantee you that nobody else has. We spend just that little more to get the products that our customers desire. If you shop with us, We can guarantee you a shopping experience like no other.
