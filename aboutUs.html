History

Anime Oasis started back in April 2020 when the pandemic hit. I wanted to get a place that I was able to shop to since I couldn't go outside for a couple of months.First, me and my small team started out in a warehouse where we started to go with small shipments and expanded from there. Here at Anime Oasis, we strive ourselves with excellent customer service and exceptional delivery time of your product.
